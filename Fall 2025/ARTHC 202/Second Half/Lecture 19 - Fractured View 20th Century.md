## Picasso & Braque
#### Pablo Picasso, First Communion, 1896 
- Picasso didn't just paint abstract paintings
- He did highly illusionistic and academic paintings
	- He just wasn't "in" to it
- Super talented artist that got bored of it 
	- became an artistic sponge
#### Picasso, Old Guitarist, 1905
- Blue period
	- Picasso in an expressionist mode
	- blue
	- art is pretty melancholy
- exaggeration and elongation of a man
#### Picasso, Family of Saltimbanques, 1905
- Rose period
	- uses colors in a rose
	- Emotionally ambiguous paintings
#### Picasso, Les Demoisselles D'Avignon, 1907
- not a truly cubist work
- transitional piece
- adaptation of the rose period
- Still in an expressionist mode 
- Saw the paintings of Cezanne
	- flattened landscapes
